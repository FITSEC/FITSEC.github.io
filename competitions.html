Competitions


CSAW CTF Qualifier

CSAW CTF is an entry-level CTF hosted by NYU every year in the fall. Categories include Pwnable, Reverse Engineering, Web exploitation, Cryptography, and Forensics. For more information follow the link below.
CSAW CTF

Southeastern Collegiate Penetration Testing Competition Qualifier

The CPTC is a competition that focuses on network security auditing. It mimics a real-life penetration test for students to test their skills. At the end of the competition the team writes a report based on their findings. Additionally, competitors can be asked to perform various assessments in addition to their primary role of penetration testing. See the link below for additional information.
National CPTC

Raymond James CTF

Raymond James CTF is an invitational CTF hosted by Raymond James in Tampa, Florida. In addition to competing against other collegiate teams, the FBI and Secret Service attend the competition as well. This competition is heavily forensics based. This CTF is used as a recruiting tool for Raymond James.

Duel Factor CTF

Duel Factor CTF is hosted annually at the Florida Cyber Conference. This CTF is more penetration testing and forensics oriented. For information on last year's CTF please see the link below.
Duel Factor CTF

HiveStorm Defensive CTF

HiveStorm is a cybder defense competition for college students. The competition is four hours long. You are provided 5 machines to patch, reconfigure, and remove malware from to be more secure. For more information on HiveStorm see the link below.
HiveStorm Defensive CTF

Department of Energy Cyberforce Competition

The DoE Cyberforce Challenge is a blue teaming event where competitors are given a compromised network where they have to harden it before the competition During the competition, the teams work together to keep critical services up. A red team will actively try to bring down services, deface websites, and cause problems for the blue team. The competitors are scored on their ability to keep the systems up, react to injects, and defeat red team attacks. For mor information see the link below.
DoE Cyberforce Challenge

Southeastern Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition Qualifier

CCDC is a defensive event where competitors are given a compromised network that they must take back from the attackers and set up defensive measures to keep them out. For more information see the link below.
CCDC

Sunshine CTF

Sunshine CTF is an online CTF but also has an on-site CTF as well. This CTF is hosted in conjunction with B-Sides Orlando security conference. This is a fairly standard jeopardy style CTF.

National Cyber League

The NCL is an offensive and defensive based CTF that has individual and team competitions throughout the competition season. For more information see the link below.
NCL
